Design & Illustration

I have over 3 years experience using Adobe Creative Cloud, and over 2 decades of experience as a traditional artist. I use a combination of everything I have learned to provide artwork, and designs of the highest quality, that I am proud of.

Photo / Video & Editing

I use Adobe Premire Pro & After Effects to create small films and edit media. I have over 2 years experience with video editing. I also use Adobe Photoshop to edit, restore and/or manipulate photography to help achieve the best results for your photos.

Other Projects

I also enjoy doing a variety of other physical medias, such as costuming and prop making. I use a 3D printer to make highly detailed recreations of your favorite Movie/TV props and then hand paint each one for a highly detailed finish.

Who am I ?

I'm a success story . . .

I grew up in a small town here in NC and I always loved to draw as a kid. After receiving my GED, in 1995, I went on to do various jobs. I was a carperter for a little while. I also worked in several retail stores, and even owned my own small business. Then the pandemic happened. . .

So I decided to do something scary. I went back to school to chase a dream I had given up on a long time ago. I was gonna see if I still had what it took to be an artist! Through lots of hard work, dedication, and the support of my family (and a few good friends), I was a success. I AM A SUCCESS!
